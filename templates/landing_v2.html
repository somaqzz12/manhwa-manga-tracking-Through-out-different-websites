How it works

1) Search title or paste URL → 2) Compare source websites and recommendation → 3) Add to library and track progress.

Supported URLs become automatic, unsupported URLs become manual tracking, and requested domains become future adapters.

Safety & open-source note

This project tracks metadata and source URLs only. It does not host manga pages or chapter images, and does not bypass paywalls or protected access controls.
