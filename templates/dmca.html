DMCA / Takedown

Manga Watchlist stores metadata and source links. It does not host manga chapters, pages, or panel images.

If you are a rights holder and want us to remove or block specific source links or metadata references, send a request that includes:

  • Your name and contact information.
  • The URL(s) and title(s) you want reviewed.
  • A statement of your rights and why the material should be removed/blocked.
  • A good-faith statement that the notice is accurate.

Contact: takedown@mangawatchlist.local

General policy: we review requests, may disable affected source links, and keep manual tracking available where legally appropriate.
