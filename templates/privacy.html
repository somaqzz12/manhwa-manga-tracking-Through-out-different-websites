Privacy

How Manga Watchlist handles account data, tracking metadata, and third-party source links.

What we store

Account data (username/email and password hash), your library entries, source URLs, cover URLs when public, latest chapter labels/URLs, and your reading progress.

We do not host manga chapters/pages or panel image files.

Cookies and sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and protect account actions. These cookies are required for account features and are not used for ad tracking.

Retention and deletion

Library metadata stays until you delete entries or your account. You can request deletion by using account settings or contacting us through the takedown/contact links below.

Third-party sites and external links

When you add or refresh source URLs, the app may fetch public page metadata from third-party domains. Those sites may log requests as part of normal web traffic. External source links open outside Manga Watchlist and are not controlled by us.

Users are responsible for the URLs they add. Prefer official/licensed sources where possible.

Extension permissions summary

The browser extension requests limited permissions (for example active tab, storage, and supported-site scripting) to collect tracking metadata. It is designed for metadata capture only and does not proxy/download manga chapter images.
