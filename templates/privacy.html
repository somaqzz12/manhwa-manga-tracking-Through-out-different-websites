Privacy

What this app stores and how credentials are handled.

Account data

  • Username — your chosen login name.
  • Password — stored only as a hash (one-way). Plain-text passwords are never stored.
  • Email — a synthetic local address may be generated from your username for legacy schema compatibility.

Library data

  • Bookmarks — series title, canonical URL, cover URL, last check time, latest chapter metadata, and optional error messages from scrapes.
  • Reading progress — chapter numbers and labels you mark as read, tied to your account.

Covers and scraping

To refresh chapter counts and artwork, the server fetches pages you bookmarked. Those third-party sites may log requests (including IP) like any normal browser visit.
